Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know the right course for me

You can find out the best course for you to study by browsing through the courses page of the website and reading what each course is all about.

Is the programme free

Yes, the programme is free

Will I pay for certificate at the end of the programme

Certificate is free. At the end of the programme, the soft copy of your certificate will be forwarded to your email address

What is the duration for the completion of the programme

The duration of the programme depends on the course studied.
The courses duration is between 3 - 6 months.

Who is eligible for the programme

Anyone from the age of 13 is eligible for the programme.

Is there any assessment to be take during the course of the programme?

Yes. Assessment is taken every 2 weeks till completion of the programme in order for certificate to be awarded.

Is there a fee to be paid for registration

There is no registration fee to be paid. The programme is totally free

Do I need a laptop for the programme

Yes. Although some of the courses can be learnt without a laptop, it is advisable to have one so as to make learning easier

Can I study more than one course at a time

Yes, you can study more than one course at the same time. However, you are not allowed to study more than 3 courses at the same time.

How do I track my progress during the programme

You can track your progress my taking the assessments provided at the end of each lecture
